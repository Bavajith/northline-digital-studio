Contact FAQ

Common questions before sending an inquiry.

This section helps answer concerns before someone fills out the form.

Do I need to know exactly what I want?

No. You can share your business type, goals, and examples of websites you like. We can help shape the structure and direction.

Can you work with an existing website?

Yes. We can review an existing website and recommend whether it needs a refresh, redesign, rebuild, or smaller improvements.

Can this be built in WordPress?

Yes. WordPress is a strong option if you want editable pages, blogs, plugins, and content management after launch.

What if I do not have photos or text yet?

We can start with placeholder content and help plan what photos, text, sections, and business details are needed before launch.

How quickly can a website be launched?

Simple websites can often be launched faster than larger builds. Timeline depends on content readiness, number of pages, features, and review speed.
