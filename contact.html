Contact FAQ

Common questions before sending an inquiry.

These answer the usual concerns business owners have before starting a website project.

Do I need to know exactly what I want?

No. You can share your business type, goals, and a few website examples you like. We can help shape the structure and direction.

Can you work with an existing website?

Yes. We can review the current website and recommend whether it needs a refresh, redesign, rebuild, or smaller improvements.

Do I need to manage hosting or deployment?

No. We handle the technical launch and deployment. You should own your domain, and we can help connect it to the finished website.

What if I do not have photos or text yet?

We can start with placeholder content and help plan what photos, text, sections, and business details are needed before launch.

How quickly can a website be launched?

Simple websites can launch faster than larger builds. Timeline depends on page count, content readiness, features, and review speed.
