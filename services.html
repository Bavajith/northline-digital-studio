Why custom matters

Template builders can launch a page. Custom work shapes the experience.

Wix, GoDaddy, and basic templates can be useful starting points, but many small businesses outgrow them when they need stronger branding, cleaner mobile layouts, better structure, and a more professional first impression.

Basic template site

Often works as a quick placeholder, but can feel generic, crowded, or disconnected from how the business actually needs to sell.

  • Limited layout flexibility
  • Can look similar to other businesses
  • Mobile sections may feel squeezed
  • Lead flow is often an afterthought

Northfolk custom build

Designed around the business, the customer journey, the mobile experience, and the action you want visitors to take.

  • Custom page structure and stronger visual direction
  • Mobile-first sections built for real phone users
  • Lead capture, WhatsApp, forms, and contact paths planned upfront
  • Cleaner code, lighter structure, and stronger launch setup
