Build options

WordPress or custom build? We recommend based on the client’s needs.

Different businesses need different approaches. A simple local business may not need an advanced custom stack. A growing brand may need more flexibility.

WordPress Build

  • Great for clients who want to edit content
  • Useful for blogs, service pages, and CMS control
  • Works well with plugins and integrations
  • Common choice for local business websites
  • Good for long-term content updates

Custom Build

  • Great for highly polished, fast websites
  • More control over design and performance
  • Good for landing pages and premium brand sites
  • Less plugin dependency
  • Strong option for modern, lightweight websites
