Build options

Custom code or WordPress? We choose based on the business.

Not every business needs the same setup. The right option depends on how often the site changes, how much control is needed, and the client’s comfort level.

WordPress

Best when the client wants editable content, pages, posts, plugins, or long-term content flexibility.

  • Editable pages
  • Good for blogs and growing service pages
  • Plugin and CMS flexibility
  • Useful for clients who want content control

Custom Build

Best when the priority is polished presentation, speed, lightweight structure, and design control.

  • Fast and lightweight
  • Strong design control
  • Good for landing pages and business sites
  • Less plugin dependency
