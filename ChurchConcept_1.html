Mission & Legacy

Rooted in Toronto. Reaching beyond borders.

Since 1995, THMC has grown as a church committed to worship, discipleship, outreach, and mission. The ministry continues to serve families across the GTA while supporting mission work internationally.

Through Blessing Hands Mission, the church supports outreach, training, and practical care for communities in need.
