Pricing FAQ

Questions clients usually ask before choosing a package.

This section helps reduce pricing confusion and makes the next step easier.

Are these fixed prices?

No. These are starting prices. Final pricing depends on the number of pages, content needs, design complexity, platform, integrations, and timeline.

Do clients pay everything upfront?

A common structure is 50% deposit to start and 50% before launch. Larger projects can be split into milestones.

Is hosting included?

Hosting can be included in a maintenance plan or billed separately depending on whether the site is built on WordPress, custom hosting, or another platform.

Can the client update the site themselves?

Yes, if the site is built in WordPress or another editable CMS. For custom static websites, updates can be handled through a maintenance plan.

What package should most local businesses choose?

The Growth Website is usually the best fit because it includes enough pages, trust-building sections, and lead-generation structure for most local businesses.
