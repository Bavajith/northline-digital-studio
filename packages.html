Questions

Common questions before starting.

The goal is to make the process clear, realistic, and easy to understand before any project begins.

Are these fixed prices?

No. They are starting prices. Final pricing depends on page count, content, features, integrations, timeline, and overall complexity.

Do I need to understand hosting or deployment?

No. We handle the technical launch and deployment. You should own your domain, and we can help connect it to the finished website.

Who owns the domain?

The client should own the domain. This keeps ownership clear and avoids future issues. We can guide you through purchasing and connecting it.

Is monthly maintenance required?

No. Monthly maintenance is not required. Future edits and updates can be requested separately when needed.

Can I request changes after launch?

Yes. Small post-launch adjustments can be handled after the site goes live. Larger changes or new pages can be quoted separately.

What package fits most local businesses?

The Growth Website is usually the best fit because it gives enough space for services, trust signals, calls-to-action, and inquiry flow.
