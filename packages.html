Pricing FAQ

Common questions before choosing a package.

The goal is to keep pricing simple while still giving enough flexibility for different business needs.

Are these fixed prices?

No. They are starting prices. Final pricing depends on pages, content, features, platform, timeline, and complexity.

What package fits most local businesses?

The Growth Website is usually the best fit because it gives enough space for services, trust signals, calls-to-action, and inquiry flow.

Do clients pay everything upfront?

A common structure is a deposit to start and the remaining balance before launch. Larger projects can be split into milestones.

Can the website be edited after launch?

Yes. WordPress sites can be edited by the client. Custom static sites can be updated through a care plan.
