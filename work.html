How to read the work

Every project tests a different business problem.

The portfolio is meant to show direction, structure, and taste. It gives local businesses a way to imagine how their own brand could be presented with more clarity and polish.

01

Industry fit

A church should not feel like a contractor. A bakery should not feel like a real estate site.

02

Clear path

Each concept is structured around what visitors need to understand before they contact the business.

03

Visual trust

Spacing, typography, color, and hierarchy all affect whether a business feels credible.

04

Practical launch

The final site still needs to work on mobile, load properly, support forms, and be easy to maintain.
